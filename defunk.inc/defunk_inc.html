About Us

Defunk.inc is a pioneering biotech startup dedicated to developing innovative anti-dependency drugs. Our mission is to eradicate the debilitating signs and symptoms of drug dependency caused by substances such as heroin, cocaine, and other harmful drugs.

Our Product

Our product, available in injectable or oral forms, is designed to prevent dependency and its associated effects. It neutralizes the addictive substance in the body and normalizes hormone levels to mitigate severe withdrawal symptoms.
